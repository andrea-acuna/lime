What is Lime?

Lime is a leading global micromobility company offering shared electric vehicles for sustainable urban transportation. The platform provides on-demand access to electric scooters, bikes, and mopeds through a simple smartphone app.

Key features include:

  • Instant Access: Unlock vehicles in seconds through the Lime app
  • Eco-Friendly: Zero-emission electric vehicles reducing urban carbon footprint
  • Smart Technology: GPS-enabled fleet with advanced safety features
  • Affordable Pricing: Pay-per-minute model with no subscription required

Lime's Gen4 electric scooter - The flagship micromobility vehicle

How Does It Work?

Lime launches app-less rides and no fee reservations to get more people riding

Lime's mobility platform operates through a seamless user experience that allows anyone to:

  1. Locate: Find available vehicles on the map through the Lime app, showing real-time locations and battery levels.
  2. Unlock: Scan the QR code on the vehicle or enter its ID to instantly unlock and begin your journey.
  3. Ride: Travel to your destination safely using bike lanes and following local traffic regulations.
  4. Park & End Ride: Park responsibly in designated areas and end your ride through the app with just a tap.

Behind the scenes, Lime's operations team regularly maintains, charges, and repositions vehicles to ensure availability throughout urban areas, powered by sophisticated data analytics and fleet management technologies.

When Was It Introduced?

Lime was founded in 2017 as a dockless bike-sharing company before rapidly expanding into the broader micromobility market. The company's growth trajectory showcases the rapid evolution of urban mobility solutions.

The development timeline included:

  • 2017: Initial launch as LimeBike, offering dockless bicycles in select US markets
  • 2018: Introduction of electric scooters and expansion to over 100 cities globally
  • 2019: Launch of next-generation vehicles with enhanced safety features
  • 2020: Introduction of Lime Access program for underserved communities
  • 2022: Deployment of Gen4 scooters with improved sustainability and safety
  • 2023: Launch of advanced rider assistance systems and integration with public transit apps

This rapid evolution showcases Lime's commitment to innovation in sustainable urban transportation and its pioneering role in the growing micromobility industry worldwide.
